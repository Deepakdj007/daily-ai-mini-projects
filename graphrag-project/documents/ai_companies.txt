OpenAI was founded by Sam Altman and Elon Musk in 2015. 
OpenAI created GPT-4, which is used by Microsoft in its Copilot products.
Microsoft invested $10 billion in OpenAI in 2023.
Google DeepMind developed Gemini, which competes with GPT-4.
Anthropic was founded by former OpenAI employees including Dario Amodei.
Anthropic created Claude, which is an AI assistant focused on safety.
