India is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area and the most
populous country. New Delhi is the capital of India. The Indian economy is one of the
fastest-growing major economies. Mumbai is the financial capital and home to the Bombay
Stock Exchange. The Reserve Bank of India, headquartered in Mumbai, controls monetary policy.
Narendra Modi has served as Prime Minister since 2014. India became independent from British
rule on August 15, 1947.
